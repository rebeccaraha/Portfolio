Hello! I'm Rebecca, a Mechanical Engineer and Designer turned Human-Centered Design Researcher and Strategist!

I explore, analyze and understand how people experience products, services, and experiences and how to better design these experiences for real people.

I thrive in envrionments where I am able to be agile and have room to explore. I love working in environments that enable and support exploration and education. I always aim to continually learn from experiences, literature, and peers; while sharing my experience and expertise as well.
