Hello! I'm Rebecca,a Human-Centered Design Strategist! I like solving complex problems in simple ways with an unwavering focus on the human experience.

I explore human behavior and preference to understand how people experience products, services, and environments and develop insights on how to better design these experiences for real people by leveraging my research expertise and integrated design & engineering background. Outside of work, my passion projects focus on exploring the future of Integrated Design & Engineering Education and Design Thinking at scale.
