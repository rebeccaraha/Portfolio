Elephant Keychain

DIY Design + Fabrication - Carnegie Mellon University | Fall 2017
Independent Project

Sketches & Hand Rendering

Left: Orthographic sketches of elephant keychain.

Below: Perspective view market renderings.

For both the orthrographic sketches and perspective renderings, the lines low poly lines weren't drawn in in the final iterations. This decision was made to perserve the quality and readability/understandability of the drawings.

Solidworks

Editing Low Poly Elephant to Low Poly Elephant Keychain

As I am not proficient in Solidworks, a file was chosen from the side Cult3D to be edited.

This file was resized to be the proper dimensions and volume. Then, geometry was added to add a 'loop' to turn the elephant from a trinket to a keychain.

A before and after animation can be seen on the left.

Thickness & Stress Testing

.06" thickness check. While some areas did not meet the .06 thickness due to the taper edges, these were nencessary for the 'low poly' design of the elephant and therefore, were permissible.
