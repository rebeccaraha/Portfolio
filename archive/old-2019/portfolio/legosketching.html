Lego Sketching

DIY Design + Fabrication - Carnegie Mellon University | Fall 2017
Independent Project

The Lego Sketching exersize focused on improving my 2D and 3D sketching skills through sketching three unique lego pieces. The legos were sketched independently in orthogonal and different perspective views and as an assembly in orthogonal views and a perspective view.
